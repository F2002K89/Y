Scam Alert

We are aware of fraudulent entities claiming to represent NEFT OIL TRADING. We only communicate from @neftoiltrading.com email addresses and our official channels.

If you receive suspicious messages or offers, do not respond. Report immediately to info@neftoiltrading.com.

Protect Yourself

  • Verify the sender — official emails end with @neftoiltrading.com.
  • Never send money or share sensitive information to unknown parties.
  • Contact us directly if you are unsure of a communication’s legitimacy.
  • Report scams to us and local authorities.
