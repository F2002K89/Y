⚠ Scam Alert

Beware of fraudulent individuals or entities falsely claiming to represent NEFT OIL TRADING FZE.

Common Fraud Methods

  • Fake offers or contracts using our company name and logo.
  • Emails from unofficial domains pretending to be NEFT employees.
  • False investment or product supply requests requiring upfront payment.
  • Social media or WhatsApp messages offering jobs, fuel, or crude oil deals.

Official Communication Channels

All official emails from NEFT OIL TRADING end with @neftoiltrading.com.

Our only verified contact details are listed on the Contact page of this website.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Verify email addresses and phone numbers before sharing information.
  • Do not send payments to individuals claiming to represent our company.
  • Always confirm transactions through our official email or office number.
  • If you suspect a scam, contact us immediately via info@neftoiltrading.com.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you receive any suspicious messages, documents, or offers claiming to be from NEFT OIL TRADING FZE, please report them by forwarding to info@neftoiltrading.com. Include screenshots or attachments if possible.
